What is Buzzarm card?

Robust yet thin, Buzzarm is a small electronic tracking and warning device
that hides inside valuable items. Buzzarm then syncs up with your smartphone to set off
an alarm if there’s a theft attempt and give you GPS coordinates if the item is lost.

How it works?

Buzzarm has five main features

Anti-theft – If someone tries to steal something with
Buzzarm inside, the device will sound an alarm.

Loss Protection – Buzzarm will notify your smartphone
Bwhen the distance between you and your device
is greater than a specified range.

Retrieval – If something is lost, Buzzarm will report
GPS coordinates to your smartphone so you can track
it down.

Law Enforcement – Buzzarm stores information
on theft attempts for you to communicate with police.

Safety Mode – You can pause Buzzarm at anytime
or use a smart watch to handle the device.

Touch alert

Buzzarm can sense body movement and if
anything gets too close will set off an alarm.
